## Project Overview

- **Application**: Legistalation Navigation & Compliance Tool
- **Challenge Categories**
  - Connecting New Citizens to Australian Democracy
  - Community AI Agents: Bridging Service Access Gaps
  - The Red Tape Navigator
  - Using AI to Help Australians Navigate Government Services
  - Making AI Decisions Understandable and Clear
- **Timeline**: 3 days (Friday 7pm - Sunday 5pm)

### Problem Statement

Australian legistative and government systems often fail to accommodate the diverse levels of education and language proficiency among its citizens. This leads to significant barriers in accessing essential services, understanding legal obligations, and fully participating in democratic processes.
